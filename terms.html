Support Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: April 02, 2026

These terms outline the support relationship between Ruberto IT Solutions and the Client. By utilizing the KK Pharmacy Management System, the Client agrees to the following:

1. Standard Support Hours

Technical support is available during the following windows:

  • Monday - Friday: 08:00 to 17:00
  • Saturday: 09:00 to 13:00 (Remote Only)
  • Public Holidays/Sundays: Emergency Only

2. What is Covered?

The monthly maintenance fee covers:

  • Software bugs and database errors within the PMS.
  • Medical Aid switch connectivity troubleshooting.
  • Daily encrypted cloud backup management.
  • Minor system updates and security patches.

3. What is NOT Covered?

The following items may incur additional "Out-of-Scope" hourly labor charges:

  • Physical damage to hardware (drops, liquid spills, power surges).
  • Third-party software installation (Social media, non-business apps).
  • New hardware installation or major physical office relocation.
  • Recovery from damage caused by unlicensed personnel tampering with the server.

4. Client Responsibilities

To ensure system stability, the Client agrees to:

  • Ensure the server is connected to a functioning Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS).
  • Maintain a stable internet connection for Medical Aid processing.
  • Not share administrative passwords with unauthorized third parties.
Emergency Protocol: For critical system outages (Pharmacy cannot dispense), please use the Priority Support WhatsApp line for immediate 1-hour response.
