System Problem?

Follow this logic before calling Support.

Is the PC screen blank/black?
YES NO
Check the UPS (Power Backup).

Is the UPS beeping or showing a red light? Ensure the plug is firm in the wall socket.

Is the "Medical Aid Verify" failing?
YES
Check the VPN Light on the Router.

If the blue light on the MikroTik is flashing, wait 2 minutes for the tunnel to reset. Do not unplug the router.

Labels not printing?
Reset the Zebra Printer.

Switch OFF, wait 10 seconds, switch ON. Ensure the green "Status" light is solid, not flashing.
